Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How Can I use Web-Chat?

Visit the Web-Chat homepage, once you fill-in the form you click on the “Start Chat” button. There is also an option of becoming a VIP member.

Sending a message to an online user, how is it done?

After you login to Web-Chat chat, on your Middle you will see the list of all online users. Select the one you want to chat with by clicking his/her name, where the chat box will open for you to write a message in it. Finish by clicking on the “send” button to send it.

How safe is Web-Chat?

It is important for you to visit the safety tips page before doing anything after joining Web-Chat. However, Web-Chat Development Team and Administration have gone to lengths of ensuring safety by having the capability to block some countries and IPs with a bad repute.

If I have a problem, how can I contact your back office support?

You can certainly go to our contact page and contact us at any time when you have an issue. We are here to help you resolve your problem and to hear you.
