Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is Web Chat secure?

Yes! All chats are end-to-end encrypted and stored securely. We prioritize user privacy and use modern security protocols.

2. Can I filter users by location or gender?

Absolutely. Web Chat allows you to filter other users based on gender, state, and online status to make your interactions more meaningful.

3. How do I report abuse?

You can report abusive users directly within the chat or contact our support team via Contact Us.

4. Do I need an account to use Web Chat?

Yes. You need to create a free account to access all messaging features, manage your profile, and maintain secure communication.

5. Can I delete my account?

Yes. You can request account deletion from your settings or contact our support team. All personal data and messages will be permanently removed.
